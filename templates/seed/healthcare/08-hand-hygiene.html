Infection Prevention

Hand Hygiene Guide

Proper handwashing is the single most effective way to prevent the spread of infection. Follow these 6 steps every time.

94%
This month
I
Wet Hands
Wet hands thoroughly under clean, running water. Turn off the tap to conserve water.
5 seconds
II
Apply Soap
Apply enough soap to cover all surfaces of your hands. Use liquid or foam soap from the dispenser.
3 seconds
III
Lather & Scrub
Rub palms together, then scrub backs of hands, between fingers, and under nails vigorously.
20 seconds
IV
Rinse Well
Rinse hands thoroughly under clean running water. Ensure all soap residue is completely removed.
10 seconds
V
Dry Hands
Dry hands completely using a clean paper towel or air dryer. Moist hands spread more germs.
10 seconds
VI
Turn Off Tap
Use a paper towel to turn off the faucet and open the door to avoid recontamination.
5 seconds
Clean Hands Save Lives
Wash hands before and after patient contact, before procedures, and after touching surroundings
