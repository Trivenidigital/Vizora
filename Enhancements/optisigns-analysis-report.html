1. Platform Overview

Future Signage is a cloud-based digital signage management platform enabling businesses to manage and distribute content to display screens across locations. The platform uses a SPA-based web dashboard at Future Signage with a consistent green-themed UI and top navigation bar.

Architecture: Cloud-hosted SPA with real-time updates. Supports multiple device types including Fire TV Stick, Android TV, Raspberry Pi, Windows, Chrome OS, LG webOS, Samsung Tizen, and their own OptiKiosk hardware. Per-screen licensing model with 5 pricing tiers.

Primary Navigation

  • Screens — Device management and monitoring
  • Files/Assets — Media library and content storage
  • Playlists — Content sequence builder
  • Schedules — Calendar-based content scheduling
  • Templates — Design studio with AI generation
  • Engage — Interactive experiences (kiosks, QR, IoT)
  • Analytics — Proof of Play and AI-powered audience analytics

  • Schedule list in left sidebar with checkboxes for bulk operations
  • Search, sort, and filter controls
